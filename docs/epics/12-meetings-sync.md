# Epic 12 — Meetings & sync

**Phase:** 3 — Business surface

## Goal

Structured syncs where agents and humans align on progress and next steps, generating follow-up
work.

## Why it matters

Meetings are how the business coordinates; agents contribute updates and receive direction.

## Key stories

- Meetings as structured (initially async) threads with mixed human/agent participants.
- Agents contribute updates drawn from their work and answer questions.
- Action items become new work-board items automatically.
- (Later) real-time meeting participation.

## Acceptance criteria

- A meeting with human and agent participants produces a summary and action items that appear on
  the board as claimable work.
